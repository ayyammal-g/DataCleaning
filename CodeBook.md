Fields:

1. Subject: 
    The ID for the person who carried out the experiment
    Values 1 - 30
    
2. Activity:
    The Activity the subject was doing during the experiment. The values are
    
    WALKING
    WALKING_UPSTAIRS
    WALKING_DOWNSTAIRS
    SITTING
    STANDING
    LAYING
